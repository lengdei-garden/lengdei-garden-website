About

What is Leng Dei?

"Leng Dei" means Play with the Soil in Khmer. We are a social enterprise in Phnom Penh connecting people with soil, biodiversity, and environmental care.

Through food gardens, hands-on workshops, and a growing community of learners, we believe nature is the best teacher.

"The soil is the foundation of life. Everything that grows, grows from it."

Our 1,000m² educational food garden in the heart of Phnom Penh is a living classroom — open to everyone.

Our Story

From a balcony kit to a living garden

At Leng Dei, we believe we are growing disconnected from the materiality of our needs and from the interconnected nature of all living beings. As a result, exploited and polluted lives are at the heart of our development choices.

Leng Dei aspires to bring more fun and life quality to environmental education — empowering people to connect with soil, biodiversity and environmental care.

3 mission channels

1

An empowering space

A 1,000m² educational food garden in Phnom Penh — so people feel "Wow! I'm inspired to be part of this!"

2

Empowering educational toolkits

To compost, garden, cook, teach — so people feel "I did it! And I loved it! And many things clicked!"

3

An empowering network

For an ecologically literate world — where educators find programmes, share support, and grow together.

Started in 2019 as Compost City, winner of SmartSpark 3 at Impact Hub Phnom Penh. Since then: 500+ compost kits, 104 workshops, 1,600+ people trained.
